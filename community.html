Community Guidelines

Our community is built on mutual respect, privacy awareness, and shared commitment to digital rights. We ask all members to:

  • Respect others' privacy and security preferences
  • Share knowledge and help newcomers learn
  • Report bugs and security issues responsibly
  • Maintain civil discourse in all interactions
  • Follow platform-specific rules and guidelines
